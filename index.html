How it Works

Producers Grow Money

Producers agree to contracts to sell local & organic goods to their retailer, retaolers' consumers, or to distributor partners at a future date for a fair market price. These contracts behave like money that producers can use to buy things they need for their business. So, they've just minted a money supply and got immediate access to money!

You Trade $ for OrganiCoins

Consumers exchange dollars for OrganiCoins online, using the app, or at retailer kiosks. Unlike dollars and bitcoins, OrganiCoins derive their value from abundance rather than scarcity. Also, OrganiCoins retain their purchasing power over time, that is, they are immune to the effects of monetary inflation.

Buy Local & Organic

Use OrganiCoins at your favorite local and organic retail outlets. Local & organic goods will be less expensive, and like the goods from which they derive their value, OrganiCoins also expire, promoting wealth sharing and stimulating the growth of a new economic system that is both stable, and steady-state.
